Teeth Whitening

The most popular cosmetic dental procedure in the world today is professional, in-office teeth whitening.
It is performed under carefully monitored conditions that allow for the safe, controlled, and painless application of a relatively high concentration of bleaching gel, resulting in visible (up to 8 shades) and immediate results (in 1 hour). Tooth whiteningcan last for one or more years , depending on how well you care for your teeth and if you use a home whitening product on a regular basis for maintenance.

Tips to Prevent Tooth Discoloration After TEETH WHITENING

Avoid stain-causing foods and beverages

Coffee, tea, wine, juices, curry, black soy or tomato sauce are all foods that can cause teeth staining

Use a straw

When drinking beverages that stain so that the liquid bypasses your front teeth

Quit smoking

Smoking tobacco can cause teeth to become discoloured. Eliminating tobacco can help keep your teeth bright.

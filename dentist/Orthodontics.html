Orthodontics / Braces

Orthodontic treatment is intended to improve not only the appearance of your smile, but also the health of your gums and teeth.

By seeking orthodontic treatment, you may be able to avoid severe tooth decay, gum and bone loss, uneven tooth enamel wear, and jaw joint pain.

We are confident that we can help you achieve and maintain the beautiful, healthy smile you've always desired, using everything from traditional braces to advanced technology. Never believe it is too late to achieve the smile you have always desired!

Types of orthodontic

Metal Braces

Metal braces are becoming smaller and less noticeable, with new technology making them faster and pain-free. Teenagers love the ability to customize their braces with rubber bands to personalize them.

Ceramic Braces

Ceramic braces are a popular alternative to metal braces due to their translucent appearance and are made of the same material as teeth, making it difficult to see.

Self-Ligating braces

Self-ligating braces use clips instead of elastic bands to create less friction and reduce trips to the dental clinic. Also, bracelets reduce friction and reduce visits to dental clinics.

Clear aligners

Clear plastic aligners are more comfortable than metal braces, allowing patients to take their brace out and enjoy foods that would be difficult to eat with fixed braces. Treatment is more expensive than other treatments.
